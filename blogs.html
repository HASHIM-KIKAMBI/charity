A very Lovely Welcome to Esanyu Lyabato Orphanage & Skill Center

At Esanyu Lyabato Orphanage, located in the heart of Wobulenzi on Bombo Road, Uganda, we are dedicated to transforming the lives of orphaned and vulnerable children. Our mission is to provide a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment that fosters their physical, emotional, educational, and social development.
